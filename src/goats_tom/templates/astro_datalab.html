Astro Data Lab and GOATS

GOATS can integrate with your Astro Data Lab account, allowing data products from GOATS to be sent directly to your Astro Data Lab storage.

To enable this, you need to link your Astro Data Lab account by providing your credentials. You can do this by visiting the following link:

Link your Astro Data Lab account

Once linked, after adding a Target, you can use the "Manage Data" tab and click the "Actions" button next to each data product to send files to Astro Data Lab.

Click the image to open the Astro Data Lab portal.
