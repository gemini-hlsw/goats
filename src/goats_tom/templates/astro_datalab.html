Astro Datalab and GOATS

GOATS can integrate with your Astro Datalab account, allowing data products from GOATS to be sent directly to your Astro Datalab storage.

To enable this, you need to link your Astro Datalab account by providing your credentials. You can do this by visiting the following link:

Link your Astro Datalab account

Once linked, after adding a Target, you can use the "Manage Data" tab and click the "Actions" button next to each data product to send files to Astro Datalab.
